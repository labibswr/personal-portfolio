    Hello! My name is Labib Sarwar. I am a first-year Computer Science student at Toronto Metropolitan University. I was born in Bangladesh and then immigrated to Canada when I was 5 years old. For the past 13 years, I have made Toronto my home. I have always enjoyed technology from a young age to the point where I am now pursuing studies in this field and I am loving it! I am an aspiring software engineer and have keen interests in many fields of computer science specifically but not limited to software development, machine learning, quantum computing, and data science. I have many hobbies; I enjoy sports and have competed in tournaments for volleyball, cricket, soccer, baseball, and badminton. I enjoy music and have had a few years experience playing the french horn at a young age. Throughout the years, I have practiced programming and have developed my skills in languages such as Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I am continuously looking to strive further to broaden my wings and seek out new opportunities available to me.
